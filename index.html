Meet the Dentists

I knew I wanted to become a dentist since my fifth grade science fair. For my experiment, I placed teeth in beakers of soda. I was astonished by the changes that occurred to the teeth in such a short period of time. What an eye opener! I value my relationship with each and every one of my patients and hope we develop lifelong bonds. I want you to feel happy and confident about your smile and your health.

Offered Services

Cosmetic Dentistry

This method of professional oral care focuses on improving the appearance of your mouth, teeth, gums, and overall smile. Common procedures include teeth whitening, veneers, fillings, and implants.

Diagnostic Services

Basic services include coverage for diagnostic and preventative treatments, such as examinations, x-rays, cleanings, topical application of fluoride, oral hygiene.

Periodontal Treatments

This treatment concerns the jawbone, the gums, and surrounding oral tissue. Procedures include root planing, gum grafting, crown lengthening, and gingivectomies.

Orthodontic Services

These procedures deal with improper positioning of teeth, namely over bites, under bites, overcrowding of teeth, crooked teeth or a jaw misalignments. Some tools include braces, aligners, and head gear.

Prosthodontic Services

These services focus on the restoration of missing teeth. Treatments vary from bridges and implants to dentures and tooth reconstruction.

Endodontic Procedures

Endodontic procedures include treatments, such as root canals, that involve the inner tissues of the teeth,to clean out infection and save the tooth.
